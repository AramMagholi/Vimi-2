ABOUT

Vimi is a smart fruits and vegetables washer which is purposely designed for busy housewives who not only have to take care of house chores, but they also have to work. They and their family might skip eating fruits and vegetables that include a lot of minerals and vitamins throughout the day. Because of the low fruit and vegetable consumption, our team designed Vimi to help people get on track of eating healthy food with simple steps.

FUNCTIONS

Vimi has a built-in digital weight sensor which weighs whatever fruits or vegetables users place in the machine. A camera will scan them to identify variety, color, size, and ripeness. Vimi also has two built-in nozzles that spray our special natural recyclable fruit liquid washer that comes with the machine.

Washed fruit and veggies will be stored in special containers that come with the machine when the user purchases it. These containers balance the moisture, prevent nutrients, vitamins, and minerals losses which may happen with regular fridge.

The used water drains below to the chemical analyzer which determines what pesticides and non-organic materials washed off the fruits and vegetables. After that, all of the cleaning liquid runs through a multi charcoal filter to be recycled and reused for the next wash. The washer also has a self-cleaning function after each use.

Kill COMPLETELY bacteria and pesticide.

Prevent nutrients, vitamins, and minerals losses.

Keep fruits and vegetables fresh up to 8 hours if user forgets to take out.

Vimi remembers all the fruits and vegetables that it washed in one cycle and it sends the data to the Vimi app that the user can install on his/her phone. The app categorizes the fruits and vegetables and tells the user what he/she has to eat daily before they start going bad! Another great feature on this app is that it will give the user veggies recipes based on all the vegetables it washed.

HISTORY

1913

This invention was to provide a device which will thoroughly wash fruit such as oranges or lemons, and which will deliver the same in a clean condition. A further object of this invention was to provide a device of the type described in which the fruit in passing through the machine is rotated so as to expose the parts of the fruit to the cleansing action.

2014

A fruit washing machine was designed and fabricated taking into consideration the techno-economic status of the micro, small and medium scale fruit farmers who are the intended users of the machine. The test result showed that the washing capacity was 0.0163 tonnes/h or 16.3 kg/h and washing efficiency was 62.5 %.

2018

A fruit washing machine was designed in 2018 which mostly focused on a bigger scale, meaning that it would be beneficial for the restaurants and industrial industries like factories. A small scale fruit washer was developed and tested for tomatoes and oranges. The preliminary tests carried out on the prototype indicate a satisfactory performance.

FOUNDERS

ARAM MAGHOLI

THY NGUYEN

Vimi was created in 2026 by two designers Aram Magholi and Thy Nguyen. The idea started from an unhealthy eating habit of many people in modern days when they are too busy with work that they don’t take much care of themselves or their family. Many people forget about consuming vitamins by eating fruits and vegetables every day because it takes time to wash, or the fruit and vegetables may go bad like every other day when they are left in the fridge for so long. The main goal of this smart fruit washer is helping busy people to get on track with their daily vitamin consumption through an app as a friendly reminder without worrying about pesticides and harmful chemicals , especially busy housewives who always want the best nutritions for their family. Different from other fruit washers available on the market, the smart Vimi washer was designed to be more convenient and functional for users while keeping the best quality of the fruit and vegetables. Vimi was created to be a friend in modern families and we hope we would bring the best product and service for our users.
